Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Operator: Grudge Studio (Racalvin The Pirate King)


What we collect:

  • Your Puter account UUID (used to identify your arena account)
  • Character data you create (name, race, class, match history)
  • Game session metadata (connection times, match results)

What we don't collect:

  • Passwords (Puter handles authentication — we never see your password)
  • Payment information
  • Personal information beyond what Puter provides
  • Tracking cookies or advertising data

Data storage: Character and match data is stored in the game server database. Your Puter UUID links your browser session to your arena account. No data is sold or shared with third parties.


Cookies: This site does not use tracking cookies. Session data is stored in browser memory only and is cleared when you close the tab.


Third-party services:

  • Puter (authentication) — puter.com/privacy
  • Google Fonts (typography) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare (CDN/security) — cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

  • Not harass other players
  • Accept that accounts may be reset or wiped during development
  • Accept that this is a free service with no uptime guarantees
